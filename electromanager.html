Electromanager

Role

  • - Personal project

Year - 2017

Overview

An electricity bill calculator designed to help you estimate, optimize, and save on your energy costs. It allows you to track your monthly and yearly electricity consumption, taking into account all household appliances, so you can better manage your budget and reduce unnecessary expenses.

Live charts

Visualize your appliances power consumption with live updating charts and identify the items contributing the most to your bill.

Calibrator

Easy to use and customisable calibrator makes for instant consumption and cost results.

Customisable rates

Highly customisable electrical tariff rates makes this app suitable for a wide range of countries.
